About Me

Let me introduce myself.

I am an automation developer from Krakow, Poland. I started with web technologies (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap & JavaScript) about 5 years ago. I started with VBA & Power Query to boost my work efficiency during my work in the financial field and then moved to technologies like Power Automate Desktop, Power Automate, SQL, and Python.

My journey in automation began with a fascination for efficiency. Over the years, I've honed my skills in crafting intelligent systems that not only automate tasks but optimize workflows, enhancing productivity and driving transformative change.

What fuels my dedication to this field is the belief that automation isn't just about cutting down time—it's about unlocking untapped potential. It's about empowering teams to focus on innovation by freeing them from repetitive tasks and enabling businesses to scale and adapt in a dynamic landscape.

As an automation developer, I see every challenge as an opportunity to innovate. I'm excited to continue exploring the frontiers of automation, pioneering new solutions, and shaping a future where efficiency and innovation go hand in hand.
